Welcome to ICEODIS 2026

The 1st edition of the International Conference on Electronic, Optical Devices and Intelligent Systems (ICEODIS2026) aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, and academics to share their latest findings and ideas. Hosted in Taza, Morocco, this symposium fosters interdisciplinary dialogue on the evolving landscape of data science, AI, and system design.

About the Venue

The city of Taza, located between the Rif and Middle Atlas mountains, offers a unique blend of history and natural beauty. The conference venue is easily accessible, with accommodation options nearby and cultural attractions for international guests.
